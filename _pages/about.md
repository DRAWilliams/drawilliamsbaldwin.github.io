I am a Research Fellow at the University of Manchester, working at the Jodrell Bank Centre for Astrophysics. My work focuses on radio observations of compact astrophysical objects and their energetic outflows.

My research sits at the intersection of:

- Accretion physics  
- Jet launching mechanisms  
- Time‑domain radio astronomy  

I use facilities including **e-MERLIN**, **EVN**, and **MeerKAT** to investigate extreme astrophysical environments and their variability over a range of time scales.

Outside research, I contribute to observatory operations and software development, helping to optimise data pipelines and observing strategies for large radio arrays.
